MySpotifyInsights | React + Flask Web App

This is my most recent build, where I led a team of 4 students in creating a large-scale web application made with React and Flask. We utilized the Spotify Web API, allowing users to view their personal Spotify listening data such as top tracks, artists, albums, audio features, and also gives song recommendations based on their listening habits. Normally, Spotify only allows users to see their Spotify stats or "Spotify Wrapped" at the end of the year, however with MySpotifyInsights, users may view their listening data any time they chose.
